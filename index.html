About Us

Welcome to Just Radio, your number one source for the best music. We are dedicated to giving you the very best of radio, with a focus on great music, top-notch quality, and listener engagement.

Founded in 2023, Just Radio has come a long way from its beginnings. When we first started out, our passion for music drove us to start our own radio station, and gave us the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online radio. We now serve listeners all over the world and are thrilled to be a part of the vibrant online radio community.
